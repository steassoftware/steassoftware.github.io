About Us

The mission of Go Green Illinois Schools is to raise environmental awareness and to inspire parents, administrators, teachers, students, and community members to take action to help the environment in our local elementary and high schools. We discuss environmental efforts and issues in our schools, share ideas, and provide a network of support for one another.
